The Resto Restaurant

The perfect place to enjoy the life and food.

Resto Restaurant & Bar is a traditional European cuisine venue, servingthe best dishes and drinks from across the range of European cuisines. A definitive neighborhood joint in Davis Square, Resto offers a laid-back dining experience in a casual setting to guests from all walks of life. Honest food and honest drinks set the foundation of our place's menu!
